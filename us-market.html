US Market for SMRs, Fission, Fusion

  • The US market for SMRs is valued at $1.5 billion, driven by demand for reliable, low-carbon energy.
  • Key players include NuScale Power, TerraPower, and X-energy.
  • Fusion energy research is advancing with significant investments from both public and private sectors.

Production/Cost Analysis

  • SMRs offer lower initial capital costs and shorter construction times compared to traditional reactors.
  • Fusion power faces high R&D costs and complex engineering requirements.
  • The overnight capital cost of constructing an SMR ranges from $2,000 to $6,000 per kilowatt.

US Policy Analysis

  • The Bipartisan Infrastructure Law (2021) provides a $6 billion subsidy for nuclear plants.
  • The Inflation Reduction Act (2022) offers tax credits for zero-emission nuclear.
  • The Advanced Reactor Demonstration Program (ARDP) supports SMRs.

Overview of Global Market vs. US

  • The global SMR market is expected to reach $13.4 billion by 2032.
  • Countries like the UK, China, and Russia are leading in SMR development.
  • The US market is driven by strong regulatory support and private sector involvement.

VC Investment

  • Venture capital investment in nuclear projects has exceeded $3.4 billion in the past year.
  • Key investments include TerraPower, Commonwealth Fusion Systems, and Helion Energy.
  • Fusion startups are attracting significant funding due to recent breakthroughs.
