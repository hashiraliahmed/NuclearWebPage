Technology Applicable to Review: SMRs, Fission, Fusion

  • Small Modular Reactors (SMRs): A class of small nuclear fission reactors designed for modular construction and flexible deployment.
  • Fission: The process of splitting atomic nuclei to release energy, used in traditional nuclear reactors.
  • Fusion: The process of combining atomic nuclei to release energy, similar to the reactions powering the sun.
